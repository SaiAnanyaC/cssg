Carnatic Music

I learn a form of Indian Classical Vocal Music style known as Carnatic Music. I have been taking music lessons for this style of music since I was a child, and it has become an integral part of who I am, taught me a lot of lessons both related to music and beyond, opened me to new opportunities, and gave me a vocal foundation to learn other styles of music such as choir. I have given multiple performances, both as a solo and in a group, in local cultural and music events.

Note: I intend on compiling photos and videos of most of my performances in this portfolio eventually. My goal is to learn how to create a gallery/slideshow element so people can slide through the different performances I have done.

Bharatanatyam

I learn a form of Indian Classical Dance style known as Bharatanatyam. I have also been taking dance lessons for this style since I was a child and it has helped me develop who I am as a person, gave me a foundation to learn other dance styles, and has also opened me to new opportunities. I have given multiple dance performance, primarily in small or large groups, in local and cultural events.

Note: I intend on compiling photos and videos of most of my performances in this portfolio eventually. My goal is to learn how to create a gallery/slideshow element so people can slide through the different performances I have done.
